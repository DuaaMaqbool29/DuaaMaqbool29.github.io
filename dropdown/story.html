ABOUT DUAA

Hi! I am Duaa and I am a HUGE foodie. I love to cook, I love to eat, and I love sharing what I love with the world.

I was born in Pakistan, a country with some of the best street food in the world. When I think back to my childhood, I remember all the fantastic restaurants my dad brought me to when I was young. You see, my family members are also huge foodies – we used to wander around night markets every weekend and skip the last period of school to try out new restaurants. Every summer, we would travel around Pakistan, exploring all the new “must-try” dishes around the country.

Cooking started to become my therapy, my stress reliever. After a long day, all I wanted to do was go grocery shopping and cook in the kitchen for hours. One year during the reading break, I made so much food I had to invite friends over to help me eat it all.

Thank you so much for supporting me and loving all my recipes.
